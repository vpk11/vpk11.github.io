Revolut — HUF Account
Pays from HUF balance automatically in Hungary. Pre-load HUF on weekdays for best rates. Avoid weekend conversions (1% fee on Standard plan).
100E Airport Bus
2,500 HUF · Pay by contactless card on board · Runs 24/7 · ~40 min to Deák tér · Not valid with Budapest Card or regular tickets
Public Transport Tickets
500 HUF per single journey · Always validate on entry · Inspectors materialize from thin air — carry your ticket at all times
Documents
Carry passport + EU Blue Card at all times · Dependent visa holder also needs valid documentation · Both Germany & Hungary are Schengen — no passport control at the airport
Restaurant Tip
Don't say "thank you" until you get change back — it means you don't want change. Check Hungarian vs English menu prices. Tip 10–15% if happy.
Airport Overnight
No lounge access needed — Terminal 2B has 24h McDonald's and seating. Bring a neck pillow. 100E bus runs all night, catch it at 21:30 from Deák tér.
Pro tip: For the Parliament sunset photo on Day 3, stand on the Pest riverbank between Chain Bridge and Margaret Bridge. The low March sun hits the Parliament façade from the west around 18:00 — golden, dramatic, and completely free.
